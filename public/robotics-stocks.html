Methodology Rules & Constraints:

  • Bottleneck Conviction: We prioritize companies that own a specific, non-substitutable mechanical or sensing chokepoint.
  • Mainstream Dissemination Penalty: We favor mid-caps and regional specialists (Japan/Korea) over US mega-cap consensus trades.
  • Valuation Discipline: High scores require sustainable PE and PEG ratios. We aggressively penalize speculative multiples that exceed historical norms.
