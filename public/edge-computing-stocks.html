Methodology Rules & Constraints:

  • Physical Resilience: We favor companies whose hardware can survive "field" deployments (extreme heat, vibration, noise).
  • Edge Autonomy: Preference for on-device inference (NPU) over cloud-dependent wrappers.
  • Valuation Discipline: We aggressively penalize firms where negative earnings or excessive PEG ratios create an unfavorable risk-reward profile.

Top 3 Highest-Conviction Edge Computing Picks (Audited)

  1. Belden Inc. ($BDC): The most disciplined entry in industrial networking. Its hardened switches are the non-obvious data backbone of the factory floor.
  2. Digi International ($DGII): A high-conviction connectivity play where aggressive growth offsets a rich nominal PE.
  3. SiTime ($SITM): Owns the precision timing chokepoint, though it remains a high-risk speculative play due to negative trailing earnings.
