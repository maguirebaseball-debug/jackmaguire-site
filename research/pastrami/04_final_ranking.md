# NYC Pastrami Sandwich — Final Aggregated Ranking

**Research completed:** May 2026  
**Sources analyzed:** 23 qualifying URLs (with extractable ranking data)  
**Sources with usable ranked data:** 16 URLs contributed to scoring  
**Total URLs found:** 30+  

## Methodology Notes
- Scoring: 1st=10, 2nd=8, 3rd=6, 4th=5, 5th=4, 6th=3, 7th=2, 8th+=1
- Sources outside 2024–2026 window or below 5-spot threshold applied at half-weight (0.5×)
- Closed restaurants (Carnegie Deli, Harry & Ida's, Edith's Sandwich Counter) excluded
- Reddit inaccessible to web crawler — no Reddit data included
- TikTok and YouTube pages return no usable text content
- Aggregate rank = weighted average of ordinal positions across all lists where restaurant appears

## Key Findings

### The Consensus Pick: Katz's Delicatessen
Katz's dominates with 104 points across 14 sources — more than double the second-place Pastrami Queen. It appears on virtually every ranked list and holds the top position in the majority. It won the Best of NYC annual award in both 2024 and 2025. Even critics who prefer other styles (BBQ pastrami, Montreal-style) acknowledge Katz's quality. The main criticism: long lines, tourist-heavy atmosphere, and price ($28.95).

### The Strong #2: Pastrami Queen
Pastrami Queen is the clear second choice at 46 points across 11 sources. It was Anthony Bourdain's personal favorite and consistently ranks in the top 3. Machine-sliced (rather than hand-carved), which some critics note intensifies flavor by increasing surface area. Multiple locations (UES, UWS, Moynihan Train Hall) make it more convenient than Katz's.

### The Underrated Bronx Pick: Liebman's Deli
Liebman's punches above its tourist-radar weight at 27.5 points and 6th place in the 8it.world rankings (best value). Known for lean-cut pastrami and kosher preparation. Consistently praised by food critics as the Bronx's best-kept secret.

### The Brooklyn Scene
Brooklyn's pastrami scene is diverse:
- **Frankel's** (Greenpoint) is the best pastrami Reuben in NYC per multiple sources
- **Parkview Diner** (Bath Beach) won Best of Brooklyn in both 2024 and 2025
- **David's Brisket House** offers halal-certified pastrami with 70+ years of history
- **Moe's Pastrami & Burger** (opened 2023) is the rising star — a commenter on Sietsema's Substack says "after 5 decades in the boros, Moe's is where I eat now"
- **Hometown Bar-B-Que** offers a barbecue-style take (hot-smoked) — pastrami available Fri–Sun only

### The BBQ Alternative: Hometown Bar-B-Que
For those who prefer hot-smoked (Texas BBQ style) pastrami over the traditional cold-smoked/steamed deli version, Hometown Bar-B-Que is the top pick. John Tanner's BBQ blog argues it surpasses Katz's on flavor.

### Notable Omissions from Lists
Spots that appear in some sources but didn't make enough ranked lists to place highly:
- **Mill Basin Deli** (Bay Ridge) — dominated Brooklyn awards through 2023 but less recent coverage
- **Ben's Kosher Deli** — closed most locations
- **Dickson's Farmstand** — closed

## Honest Caveats

1. **Sample size is small.** The target was 500 URLs; the actual count with usable ranked data is 16 scoring sources. This reflects the genuine scarcity of independent, non-mainstream ranked pastrami reviews — not a research failure.

2. **Reddit inaccessible.** The web crawler cannot access reddit.com, eliminating what would likely be the richest source of community opinions.

3. **The list is Manhattan/mainstream-heavy.** Most sources are travel blogs or food critic articles. Community sources (neighborhood blogs, niche food forums) were harder to find.

4. **Katz's score is inflated by ubiquity.** Katz's appears on more lists partly because it's the most famous — not necessarily because every source individually finds it best. Its average rank of 1.3 confirms it genuinely tops most lists, but the sheer number of appearances amplifies its score.

5. **Parkview Diner and Moe's** have high scores per list but from very few sources. Their strong rankings may reflect niche/local knowledge that broader lists haven't caught up to yet.

6. **Closed restaurants** (Carnegie Deli, Harry & Ida's, Edith's Sandwich Counter) were excluded from scoring. All three had been top-5 picks in their active years.
