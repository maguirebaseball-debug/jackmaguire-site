# NYC Pastrami Sandwich Rankings — Score Tally

## Scoring System
- 1st place = 10 pts
- 2nd place = 8 pts
- 3rd place = 6 pts
- 4th place = 5 pts
- 5th place = 4 pts
- 6th place = 3 pts
- 7th place = 2 pts
- 8th place or lower = 1 pt

## Sources Used for Scoring (with rationale)
Sources excluded from scoring (noted per URL):
- Gotham Foodie (URL 2): Explicitly unordered
- 6sqft (URL 5): Explicitly unordered
- John Tanner's PQ review (URL 13): Single-restaurant focus
- Food Finance Travel (URL 20): Below 5-spot threshold
- Diner Theory Substack (URL 22): Single-restaurant focus
- Lo Times (URL 23): Below 5-spot threshold (4 spots, pre-2024)

Sources with special notes:
- Eat This NY (URLs 8 & 9): Older content (circa 2011–2015). Applied at **half-weight** (scores halved). Harry & Ida's and Carnegie Deli are closed, excluded.
- Best of NYC annual awards (URL 7): Two separate #1-place entries (2024, 2025 = 10 pts each)
- Best of Brooklyn annual awards (URL 18): Two separate #1-place entries (2024, 2025 = 10 pts each) for Parkview Diner
- World and Then Some (URL 16): Published 2021, outside 2024–2026 window. Applied at **half-weight**.
- John Tanner's BBQ Better Than Katz (URL 12): Only 3 spots. Applied at **half-weight**.
- Old Jewish Men Substack (URL 10): Value ranking (price/lb) not taste ranking. Applied at **half-weight**.
- Peter K. Bear blog (URL 21): Scale comparison, not ordinal pastrami ranking. Applied at **half-weight** and mapped Moe's as #1, Katz as #2.
- Metropolis Moving Brooklyn (URL 15): Includes non-Manhattan Brooklyn spots — all scored normally.
- A Man and His Sandwich (URL 3): Published May 2023, just outside window, but the most recent version of the page. Applied at **half-weight**.
