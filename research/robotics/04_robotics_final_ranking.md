# Final Robotics Rankings - May 2026

## Top Pick 1: THK Co., Ltd. ($THKLY)
**Conviction Score: 92/100**
The skeletal foundation of all motion-based robotics. THK's linear motion guides are the industry standard for high-precision, high-load axes. They are the ultimate volume play for the industrial robot rollout.

## Top Pick 2: Vishay Precision Group ($VPG)
**Conviction Score: 88/100**
The nervous system for humanoids. As robots move from rigid cages to collaborative spaces, the ability to "feel" force via VPG's foil strain gauges becomes a mission-critical safety and performance bottleneck.

## Top Pick 3: Hirata Corporation ($HRAXF)
**Conviction Score: 82/100**
The gatekeeper of cleanroom automation. Hirata's dominance in wafer transfer robotics makes them a primary beneficiary of the global semiconductor fabrication expansion.
