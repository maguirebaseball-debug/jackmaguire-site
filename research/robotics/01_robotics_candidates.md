# Robotics Candidates Found

## 1. Vishay Precision Group ($VPG)
- **Role**: High-precision strain gauges and load cells.
- **Bottleneck**: The "nervous system" for humanoid joints and precision balance.
- **Thesis**: Essential for manual dexterity in humanoid robots. Tier-1 supplier for leading programs.

## 2. THK Co., Ltd. ($THKLY / 6481.T)
- **Role**: Linear motion (LM) guides and actuators.
- **Bottleneck**: The "skeletal" layer for industrial automation.
- **Thesis**: Pioneered linear motion; indispensable for sliding/lifting movements in high-duty environments.

## 3. Koh Young Technology (098460.KQ)
- **Role**: 3D measurement-based inspection.
- **Bottleneck**: Yield verification in semiconductor and electronics assembly.
- **Thesis**: Sub-micron precision verification for AI hardware and smart factories.

## 4. IPG Photonics ($IPGP)
- **Role**: Fiber lasers.
- **Bottleneck**: Precision cutting and welding "engine" for materials processing.
- **Thesis**: Recovering demand as AI infrastructure buildout requires specialized high-precision manufacturing.

## 5. Hirata Corporation ($HRAXF / 6258.T)
- **Role**: Semiconductor wafer transfer robots and custom production lines.
- **Bottleneck**: Cleanroom/vacuum automation.
- **Thesis**: Critical for the expansion of 3nm/5nm fabrication facilities.

## 6. Teradyne ($TER)
- **Role**: Cobots (Universal Robots).
- **Bottleneck**: SME automation accessibility.
- **Thesis**: Dominates the collaborative robot market (50%+ share).

## 7. Samsara ($IOT)
- **Role**: Connected operations / Data layer.
- **Bottleneck**: Interoperability and sensor fleet management.
- **Thesis**: The software glue for heterogeneous robot fleets.

## 8. Cognex ($CGNX)
- **Role**: Machine vision.
- **Bottleneck**: Visual inspection and guidance.
- **Thesis**: High-margin leader in visual components for the factory floor.
