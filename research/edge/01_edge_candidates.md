# Edge Computing Candidates Found

## 1. Belden Inc. ($BDC)
- **Role**: Industrial networking and "hardened" infrastructure.
- **Bottleneck**: Data backbone for harsh industrial environments.
- **Thesis**: Pivoted from cables to high-margin switches (Hirschmann) and IIoT infrastructure.

## 2. Digi International ($DGII)
- **Role**: Mission-critical M2M and cellular gateways.
- **Bottleneck**: "Unbreakable" connectivity for edge devices.
- **Thesis**: Leader in ruggedized cellular routers for remote/extreme deployments.

## 3. Ambarella ($AMBA)
- **Role**: AI vision processors (NPU).
- **Bottleneck**: Low-power, high-performance inference at the edge.
- **Thesis**: Pure-play on AI vision (CV-series) without the NVIDIA premium.

## 4. BlackBerry ($BB)
- **Role**: QNX Real-Time Operating System (RTOS).
- **Bottleneck**: Deterministic software for safety-critical edge hardware.
- **Thesis**: The hidden "chokepoint" OS for autonomous vehicles and robotics.

## 5. SiTime ($SITM)
- **Role**: MEMS timing solutions.
- **Bottleneck**: Precision clocking/sync for 5G and high-speed industrial data.
- **Thesis**: Replacing legacy quartz with rugged, high-precision silicon timing.

## 6. Lantronix ($LTRX)
- **Role**: Out-of-band management and AI-integrated edge gateways.
- **Bottleneck**: Remote management and traffic orchestration at the edge.
- **Thesis**: Small-cap player with high exposure to the Percenti AI series.

## 7. Applied Optoelectronics ($AAOI)
- **Role**: Fiber-optic transceivers.
- **Bottleneck**: High-speed data links between edge data centers.
- **Thesis**: Essential for reducing heat and latency in edge AI networking.

## 8. Silicon Labs ($SLAB)
- **Role**: IoT semiconductors with integrated NPUs.
- **Bottleneck**: Battery-powered AI inference.
- **Thesis**: Pure-play on the hardware-software stack for low-power IoT.
