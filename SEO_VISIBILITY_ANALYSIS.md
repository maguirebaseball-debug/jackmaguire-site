## Why Google Visibility Is Low (Root Causes)

### 1. **Age of property** (created March 26, 2026 — 2.5 months old)
   - Google takes 3–6 months to establish domain authority
   - Backlink profile likely still thin
   - No historical ranking data

### 2. **Heavy reliance on social/community promotion**
   - Your traffic is driven by HN, Reddit, Twitter shares (26–49% of traffic)
   - Few backlinks from authoritative sites → slower Google indexing
   - You're in a "community-first" loop, not a "search-first" loop

### 3. **Competitive keyword landscape**
   - Most of your topics are competitive (AI, travel, restaurants)
   - "AI Job Grief" works because it's *specific* and you owned the phrase early
   - Generic topics like "AI tools" or "travel" won't rank without massive link equity

### 4. **Low E-E-A-T signals for most content**
   - No byline author bio on blog posts
   - No credentials displayed (e.g., "Senior Performance Marketing Director" on marketing essays)
   - No "About" section prominence on blog posts
   - Google cannot easily verify expertise
