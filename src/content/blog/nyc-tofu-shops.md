A ranked list of places to buy tofu across New York City, from high-volume pan-Asian retailers to small-batch traditional makers.

## Top Picks

**H Mart**
34-51 48th St, Long Island City, Queens

The most reliable option for broad tofu selection. High-volume pan-Asian turnover means fresh stock. Multiple locations across the city.

**Sunrise Mart**
SoHo

Japanese tofu-type breadth. Worth knowing if you're in lower Manhattan.

**Fong On**
Chinatown, Manhattan

In-house small-batch production plus wholesale. If you want tofu made on-site, this is the place. Historic Chinatown operation.

**Sun Hing Lung Ho Fun**
Bronx

Factory-style tofu and rice roll production. Less of a retail experience, more of a working production operation.

## Notes on the Category

H Mart dominates the convenience end of this market. If you need tofu reliably, any H Mart works. The question is whether you want to seek out something more interesting.

Fong On is the answer to that question in Manhattan. Small-batch, traditional methods, Chinatown location. It's the kind of place that's been doing this for decades and doesn't need to advertise.

For Japanese-style silken and soft varieties, Sunrise Mart is the most consistent option downtown.

This list will be updated as I work through more of the city.
