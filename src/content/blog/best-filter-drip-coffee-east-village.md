This ranking evaluates seven East Village coffee shops exclusively on the quality of their plain brewed drip coffee. No espresso, no pour-overs, no specialty drinks. Just the house drip.

## Scoring Methodology

Each shop receives two scores averaged together:

- **Google score:** Star rating multiplied by two
- **Drip sentiment score:** 0-10 based solely on plain coffee feedback from reviews

Shops with over 500 Google reviews face a "played-out penalty" (multiplied by 0.67), reflecting that heavily-reviewed locations tend to be crowded and less personal. The penalty is an acknowledgment that a shop being famous isn't the same as it being good for a quiet weekday morning.

## Rankings

**1. Everyman Espresso** (136 E 13th St) — Score: 9.0

Just under 500 reviews, avoiding the penalty. The strongest public evidence for plain filter coffee in the neighborhood. Hours: Mon–Fri 7:30am–5:30pm, Sat–Sun 8:00am–5:30pm.

**2. Ninth Street Espresso** (700 E 9th St) — Score: 6.0

Over 700 reviews triggers the penalty, which costs it the top slot. Strong Google rating and drip coffee reputation. Hours: Daily 7:00am–7:00pm. The 9pm close is useful.

**3. La Cabra** (152 2nd Ave) — Score: 6.0

Highest Google rating (4.7) of any shop on this list. Medium confidence specifically for plain drip — the emphasis here is on specialty methods. Worth trying if you're already there.

**4. Abraço** (81 E 7th St) — Score: 5.9

Over 1,100 reviews — heaviest penalty applied. Famous old-school reputation and genuinely deserved, but the crowd is real.

**5. Coffee Project New York** (239 E 5th St) — Score: 5.6

4.7 Google rating. Specialty-focused; medium confidence for plain drip specifically.

**6-7. The Bean** (Astor Place & 2nd Ave) — Scores: 5.0 & 4.7

Reliable grab-and-go options. Open 7:00am–9:00pm daily. Not exciting but consistently decent and the hours are hard to beat.

## The Practical Recommendation

If you want the best drip coffee and you're not in a rush: Everyman Espresso. If you need something before 7:30am or after 5:30pm on a weekday: The Bean or Ninth Street. If you want to try something different: La Cabra or Coffee Project.
