## How the Day Runs

Morning, 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM: Beaches open with lifeguards. Major parades step off in Bay Ridge, Rockaway, and Douglaston. Civic ceremonies occur at the Intrepid Museum and the Soldiers and Sailors Monument. Urban Park Rangers lead history walks in Grand Army Plaza and Inwood Hill Park.

Afternoon, 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM: Concerts begin at Green-Wood Cemetery and Riverbank State Park. The DanceAfrica bazaar and performances are active in Fort Greene. Day parties start at Nowadays, Time Out Market, and The DL. The Mets game commences at Citi Field.

Evening through overnight: Jazz residencies begin at Blue Note, Village Vanguard, and Smalls. Comedy clubs host multiple holiday lineups. Late-night parties continue at House of Yes, MAMATACO, and Skyport Marina.
