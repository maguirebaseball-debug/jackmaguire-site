Danny & Coop's made the cut at 4 upvotes because the commenter promoted it unusually strongly, calling it more than just a cheesesteak and saying the peppers were essential.

Agi's Counter was called out as closing due to ongoing building problems. The owner is reportedly looking for a new space.

The thread was also prompted by Russ & Daughters' seasonal Nieuw Holland Herring sandwich ($13), available only in June. Several replies confirmed it's not comparable to the packaged herring you'll find at Tashkent or similar spots.
